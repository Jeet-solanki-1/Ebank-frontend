User App Manual

Welcome to the User App! This app allows you to easily manage your banking needs, including searching for banks, creating accounts, checking your balance, setting up your profile, and viewing all your accounts. Below is a quick guide on how to use each feature.

Features Overview

  • Search for a Bank - Find banks where you can open new accounts.
  • Create a New Account - Once you've found a bank, you can quickly create a new account with your details.
  • Check Your Balance - View the current balance in any of your accounts.
  • Set Up Your Profile - Personalize your experience by setting up or updating your profile information.
  • View All Your Accounts - See a summary of all the accounts you've created with different banks.

How to Use the Features

1. Search for a Bank

What you can do: You can search for a bank by typing its name into the search bar. This will help you find a bank quickly to create a new account.

Why it's helpful: Easily locate banks without scrolling through a long list.

2. Create a New Account

What you can do: After selecting a bank, fill out a simple form with your details and create an account with just a click.

Why it's helpful: Quickly create and manage accounts under different banks.

3. Check Your Balance

What you can do: Select an account from your list of accounts and check the available balance instantly.

Why it's helpful: Stay updated on your finances at a glance.

4. Set Up Your Profile

What you can do: You can enter your personal information (like name, email, and profile picture) to personalize the app. This helps the app recognize you and display your profile when using it.

Why it's helpful: Personalization creates a better user experience and helps keep your account details up to date.

5. View All Your Accounts

What you can do: In the "My Accounts" section, you'll see a list of all the accounts you've created. This allows you to manage multiple accounts from one place.

Why it's helpful: Get an overview of all your accounts without needing to switch between different sections.

Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to get started with the User App:

  • Open the app and navigate to the home page.
  • Use the "Search for a Bank" feature to locate a bank.
  • After selecting a bank, use the "Create New Account" option to open a new account.
  • Check your account balance using the "Check Your Balance" feature.
  • Set up your profile in the "Profile" section to personalize the app.
  • View all your accounts in the "My Accounts" section.

Additional Tips

Security: Always log out of your profile after using the app to protect your information.

Support: If you face any issues, reach out to our support team through the "Help" section in the app.
